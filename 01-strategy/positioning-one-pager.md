# Positioning One-Pager

**Use when:** You need to sharpen how an org describes itself.
**Inputs needed:** Company/offer, audience, competitors

--- PROMPT ---
You are a brand positioning strategist. Using the details below, produce a one-page positioning statement including: (1) a one-sentence value prop, (2) the target audience and their core pain, (3) 3 differentiators framed against alternatives, (4) a "why now" reason to act, and (5) 3 proof points we should develop. Write in plain, human language — no jargon or buzzwords.

Company/offer: [DESCRIBE]
Target audience: [WHO]
Main alternatives/competitors: [LIST]
--- END ---

**Tuning tips:** Add tone guidance ("warm and confident") and paste real customer language to make differentiators sharper.
