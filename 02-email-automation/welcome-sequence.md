# Welcome Email Sequence

**Use when:** Building a nurture flow for new subscribers or leads.
**Inputs needed:** Audience, offer, desired action

--- PROMPT ---
Act as an email marketing strategist. Write a 4-email welcome sequence for [AUDIENCE] who just [SIGNED UP FOR X]. Goals: build trust, deliver value, and move them toward [DESIRED ACTION]. For each email give: send timing, subject line (+ 1 alt), preview text, and body. Keep it human-to-human — conversational, one clear idea per email, one CTA. Avoid hype.

Audience: [WHO]
What they signed up for: [OFFER]
Desired end action: [GOAL]
--- END ---

**Tuning tips:** Specify sender voice and paste a past email you liked so it matches your brand.
