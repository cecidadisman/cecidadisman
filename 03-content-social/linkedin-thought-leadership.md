# LinkedIn Thought-Leadership Post

**Use when:** Turning an idea or lesson into an engaging post.
**Inputs needed:** Core idea, personal angle

--- PROMPT ---
You're a ghostwriter for a marketing leader with a candid, no-BS voice. Turn the idea below into a LinkedIn post: strong first-line hook, short punchy paragraphs, a real point of view, and a question to spark comments. No emojis unless they add meaning. No "I'm excited to share."

Core idea: [IDEA]
My personal angle/experience: [STORY OR OPINION]
--- END ---

**Tuning tips:** Ask for 3 hook variations, then pick the boldest.
